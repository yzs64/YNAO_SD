PMO - HTC Sunspot Data (HTCSD)

The aim is to provide the daily sunspot numbers, areas, umbral areas, penumbral areas of hand drawing images obtained from the Purple Mountain Astronomical Observatory (PMO) by the hybrid task cascade (HTC) method. We also provide the information of each pore, spot, umbra and hole.
